Introduce Your Toddler to Mealtime Independence with Ali+Oli Spoon & Fork Learning Set

The Ali+Oli Spoon & Fork Learning Set is the perfect tool to help your little one transition from puree to solid foods with confidence and ease. Designed specifically for babies 6 months and up, this set blends safety, durability, and comfort into a single, stylish package.

Crafted with high-quality 316 stainless steel and food-grade silicone handles, this set is built to last while ensuring your child eats safely. The ergonomic design and rounded teeth on the fork are engineered for small hands and mouths, making it easier for your toddler to grip and use the utensils effectively.

The soft silicone surfaces also help reduce the risk of choking and provide a gentle, secure grip for your child. With an anti-choke shield and safety shield, this set ensures that utensils stay in the right place during eating, making mealtime less stressful for both parents and kids.

Whether it's for breakfast, lunch, or snack time, this toddler flatware set is versatile and stylish.
