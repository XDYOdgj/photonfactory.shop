D&D Technologies ML3TPKA MagnaLatch Top Pull Gate Latch

Designed with advanced magnetic locking technology, the ML3TPKA MagnaLatch Top Pull Gate Latch offers peace of mind and superior security for your home. Perfect for pool safety and childproofing, this latch keeps your gate secure at a height that's out of reach for curious toddlers. The fully sealed magnets ensure they remain protected from weather and wear, making this an ideal choice for both indoor and outdoor applications.

Key Features

  • Adjustable vertically and horizontally for a seamless fit and future-proof installation
  • Rekeyable security lock with a clear visible indicator to confirm the latch is locked
  • Durable construction using high-quality industrial strength polymer and stainless steel
  • Easy to install and maintain for long-term reliability and performance
  • Blends with your home's exterior while providing a robust and secure closure

Why Choose This Product?

Whether you're looking for a safe solution for your backyard or a secure entrance for your business, the ML3TPKA MagnaLatch is built to last. Its rust-free design and weather resistance ensure it remains in top condition for years. The adjustable nature of this gate latch makes it adaptable to changing conditions and gate wear, while the visible lock indicator provides a level of reassurance that your property is protected at all times.

Versatile and Reliable

This top pull gate latch works with any standard square gate frame and post, offering universal compatibility. Its simple yet effective locking mechanism keeps your family and property safe without compromising on style. With minimal maintenance required, the ML3TPKA MagnaLatch is the perfect addition to any home or commercial setting where safety is essential.
