NICINGU Dinosaur Toddler Spoons & Forks Set

Make mealtime an exciting adventure for your little one with the NICINGU Dinosaur Toddler Spoons & Forks Set. Designed with both style and safety in mind, these utensils are perfect for helping your child learn to feed themselves while having fun. The set features one sturdy spoon and one fork, crafted from high-quality 304 stainless steel that is rust-resistant and free from harmful substances. The rounded, pointed tips are gentle and easy to handle, while the soft silicone handles provide a comfortable grip that's ideal for small hands.

The playful dinosaur design not only appeals to toddlers but also promotes independence, encouraging them to explore food in a safe and engaging way. These utensils are microwave and dishwasher safe, making cleanup a breeze. Each piece is individually boxed for easy storage and to maintain hygiene, ensuring your child always has the best quality baby cutlery at hand.
