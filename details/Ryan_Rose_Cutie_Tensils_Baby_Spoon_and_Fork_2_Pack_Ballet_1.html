Introduce Your Baby to Self-Feeding with the Ryan & Rose Cutie Tensils Set

The Ryan & Rose Cutie Tensils Baby Spoon and Fork Set is the ideal beginner tool for feeding your baby solid foods. Thoughtfully designed with safety as a priority, this set features a rounded fork that allows gentle scooping, reducing pressure on your baby's gums and making mealtime more comfortable for both of you.

With an ergonomic handle that fits small hands perfectly, the set supports your baby's natural grip and helps them take their first steps toward using adult utensils. This promotes confidence, motor development, and an early sense of independence in eating.

The 2-in-1 design includes both a spoon and a fork, offering versatility for different textures and types of food. Whether you're at home or on the go, these tools are perfect for making feeding times more enjoyable and stress-free.

Crafted from 100% non-toxic food-grade silicone, the Cutie Tensils Set is free from harmful substances such as BPA, PVC, phthalates, and latex. This ensures your baby's delicate mouth stays safe during each meal.
