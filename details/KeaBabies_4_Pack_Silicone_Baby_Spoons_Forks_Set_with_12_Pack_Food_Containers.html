KeaBabies 4-Pack Silicone Baby Spoons and Forks Set

Help your baby take their first steps toward independence with this essential 4-pack of baby utensils. Made from 100% pure food-grade silicone, they are safe for teething gums and gentle on delicate mouths, making them the perfect choice for your little one's growing needs.

Designed for baby-led weaning, these spoons and forks allow your child to explore different textures, such as purees, mashed foods, and solid pieces, safely and confidently. The inclusion of an anti-choke barrier ensures added security during mealtime, giving you peace of mind as they learn to eat on their own.
