Yinder 16-piece Silicone Baby Feeding Set

A must-have for parents introducing solids to their little ones. Designed with baby safety in mind, this set blends practicality with durability for effortless mealtime experiences.

Why Choose This Set?

This baby-friendly feeding set features all the essentials for introducing solids: plates, bowls, cups, cutlery, and a soft bib. Crafted from high-quality, food-safe silicone, it's gentle on your baby’s skin and easy to clean, making it ideal for a hygienic environment.

The non-slip suction design keeps plates and bowls secure on high chairs and tables, reducing mess and promoting safe exploring. Ergonomic double handles on the snack cups make it easier for your baby to grasp, encouraging independence and confidence during meals.

Key Features

  • 2 Color Options: Walnut and Beige for stylish and safe feeding.
  • 16 Pieces in One Set: Includes plates, bowls, cups, cutlery, and bib for complete mealtime solutions.
  • Suction Cup Design: Prevents sliding and keeps little hands and mess contained.
  • Soft & Flexible Material: Safe for sensitive skin and easy to clean with water or dishwasher.
  • Ergonomic Handles: Allow babies to hold cups and utensils securely while developing motor skills.

Perfect for Baby-Led Weaning

This set simplifies the transition to solid foods by offering safe and easy-to-use tools that support healthy eating habits early on. Whether it's meals or snack time, the Yinder set is built for practicality and convenience, letting both parents and babies enjoy the process.

With its durable and hypoallergenic construction, it’s perfect for use in the home or on the go. A great investment for parents who value safety, ease, and the development of self-feeding skills in their growing babies.
