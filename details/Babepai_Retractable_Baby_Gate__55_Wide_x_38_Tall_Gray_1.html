Babepai Retractable Baby Gate

A must-have safety solution for parents and pet owners, the Babepai Retractable Baby Gate is designed to protect your little ones from dangerous areas without compromising style or convenience.

With an extra wide 55\" x 38\" size, this gate offers maximum coverage to keep your baby safe from stairs, doorways, and other potential hazards. The adjustable width and height make it compatible with openings ranging from 0 to 55 inches, ensuring a perfect fit for almost any home setup.

Equipped with a double safety lock system, the Babepai Retractable Baby Gate provides secure closure while remaining easy to use. The no-bottom rail design minimizes tripping risks and allows for a clean, seamless look both indoors and outdoors. Plus, it installs effortlessly without damaging baseboards or walls, making it quick and hassle-free to set up.
