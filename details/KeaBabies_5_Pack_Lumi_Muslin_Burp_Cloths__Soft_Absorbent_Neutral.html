KeaBabies 5-Pack Muslin Burp Cloths

These muslin burp cloths are a must-have for any baby caregiver. Made from a premium blend of 70% bamboo viscose and 30% cotton, they combine softness with exceptional absorbency, perfect for catching spit-ups and messy moments during feeding times.

Each cloth measures 20" x 10", ensuring a large enough surface area for your baby's needs while still remaining lightweight and easy to manage. The 8-layer construction offers extra protection, keeping your space clean and comfortable throughout the day.

With their non-wrinkle and anti-shrink properties, these burp cloths are built to last, saving you time and money with repeated use. They're also machine washable and dryable, making them a practical choice for busy parents. The neutral color design is versatile and suitable for both baby boys and girls, adding a timeless touch to your nursery decor.

Whether you're nursing, bottle-feeding, or simply helping your baby burp, these KeaBabies burp cloths deliver the comfort and reliability you need. They're especially ideal for baby showers and registries, making a great neutral baby shower gift that’s both eco-friendly and functional.
