Our Cute Castle 12 Pack Muslin Burp Cloths

A must-have for any new parent, these burp cloths combine softness, durability, and practicality for everyday baby care.

Soft and Gentle

Handcrafted from 100% premium cotton, these burp cloths are ultra-soft, ensuring they are safe and comfortable for your baby's delicate skin. Their large 20''x10'' size is ideal for managing spills, drool, or messes during feeding and changing times.

Super Absorbent and Quick-Drying

With a highly absorbent material, these muslin cloths soak up messes fast and dry easily, making cleanup a breeze. They are perfect for both boys and girls and can be used daily without worry of fading or wear.

These burp cloths are not only great for burping but also double as a safe and hygienic changing mat cover. You can also use them as a breastfeeding cover, giving you more flexibility in baby care routines.
