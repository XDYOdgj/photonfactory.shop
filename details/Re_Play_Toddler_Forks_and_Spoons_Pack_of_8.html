Ergonomic Toddler Utensils for Safe and Enjoyable Meals

Introducing Re-Play's toddler fork and spoon set, specially designed to meet the needs of growing little ones. With 4 forks and 4 spoons in one pack, this collection combines functionality with comfort, making mealtime easier and more fun for toddlers and parents. The rounded tips on the forks and deep scoop design of the spoons ensure a secure hold and simple use, even for the most adventurous eaters.

Every piece is made from 0.2" thick, sturdy plastic that's both safe and durable, built to withstand the mess and movement that come with toddler dining. These utensils are also dishwasher and microwave safe, offering effortless cleaning that fits seamlessly into your busy routine. Having them on hand means you can handle everything from home meals to on-the-go snacks with confidence.

Why choose Re-Play?

  • Ergonomically designed for little hands, ensuring a comfortable grip and smooth use
  • Measuring 5.8" in length, 1.2" in width, and 0.2" in thickness for the perfect balance
  • Crafted from recycled materials, making them an eco-friendly choice without sacrificing durability
  • Ideal for first solid foods, daycare, or travel due to their compact and practical design
  • Ideal for both home use and on-the-go scenarios with their safe, easy-to-clean construction

These toddler eating tools are not only built to last but also made with sustainability in mind. By using recycled plastic from milk jugs, Re-Play helps reduce waste while providing high-quality, child-safe utensils for every meal. Whether you're at home or out exploring, this set is a reliable companion for your little one's growing appetite and independence.
