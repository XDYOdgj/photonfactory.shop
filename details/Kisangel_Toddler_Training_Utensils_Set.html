Kisangel Toddler Training Utensils Set

Make mealtime both safe and fun with the Kisangel Toddler Training Utensils Set. Designed with your little one in mind, these utensils are crafted from high-quality PP material, which is lightweight, non-toxic, and gentle on baby gums. The smooth, rounded edges ensure no risk of injury, making them ideal for early feeding development.

The specially contoured handles on the fork and spoon are shaped to fit the natural curve of a child's hand, helping them learn proper grip and develop fine motor skills. This set is perfect for encouraging toddlers to eat independently with confidence and comfort.

Key Features

  • Safe and durable PP material
  • Smooth surfaces with no sharp edges for baby safety
  • Bended contoured handles for comfortable use
  • Designed for easy gripping and learning to eat
  • Perfect for infants, newborns, and toddlers

Why Choose This Set?

Whether you're at home, in daycare, or on the go, these utensils make feeding time simpler and more engaging for your child. Let them explore eating with ease, comfort, and safety, while building essential self-help skills. A great choice for parents looking to support their toddler's development through everyday activities.
