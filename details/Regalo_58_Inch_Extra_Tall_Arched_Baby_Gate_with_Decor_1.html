Regalo 58-Inch Extra Tall Baby Gate

A seamless blend of safety and sophistication, the Regalo baby gate is designed to protect your home while adding a touch of elegance. Whether you're navigating a hallway or securing a doorway, this extra tall gate offers versatile fit and durable construction to meet the needs of modern families.

Key Features
  • Extra wide design fits openings from 29-58 inches for broad coverage
  • Includes 6-inch, 8-inch, and 12-inch extension kits for customizable fit
  • Sturdy metal frame with safety-lock design for reliable security
  • High-end arched design that opens both directions for added style
