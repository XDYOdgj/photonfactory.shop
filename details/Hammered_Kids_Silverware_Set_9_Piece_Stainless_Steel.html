Introducing the 9-Piece Kids Silverware Set

A perfect choice for parents looking to teach their little ones the basics of proper table manners. Designed with both safety and style in mind, this set is ideal for toddlers, preschoolers, and even school-age children.

Key Features
  • Includes 3 knives, 3 forks, and 3 spoons in a complete 9-piece set
  • Made from high-quality, non-toxic stainless steel to ensure durability and safety
  • Hammered pattern mimics adult flatware, giving kids a grown-up feel

  • Perfectly sized for small hands with rounded edges for safe handling

This set is more than just cutlery—it's a tool for developing independence during meals. The mirror-polished finish ensures smooth surfaces that are gentle on your child's mouth, while the sturdy build guarantees that it will hold up to teething and playful use.
